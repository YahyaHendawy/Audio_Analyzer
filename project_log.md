# Audio Frequency Analysis

## Goal
Convert real-world sound into measurable frequency data.

## Method
- Recorded audio using microphone
- Applied Fast Fourier Transform (FFT)
- Visualized frequency spectrum

## Results
- Voice showed mid-frequency concentration
- Clap produced wide frequency spikes
- Music showed complex multi-frequency patterns

## Key Insight
Sound is composed of multiple frequencies, and FFT allows us to break it down into measurable components.
